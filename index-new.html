MESSAGE FROM THE SECRETARIAT


Welcome Delegates!

It gives us immense pleasure and a great deal of satisfaction to bring you the seventh edition of BITSMUN, the annual international MUN conference held at BITS Pilani K.K. Birla Goa Campus from the 6th to the 8th of February, 2015. At BITSMUN, our goal is to infuse life, via academic simulation, into the thriving international diplomatic community of the United Nations. We place you in realistic scenarios to give you a deep insight into the working of the distinguished international bodies that are pivotal for the advancement of the modern world.

This year we present to you an incredible array of , enthralling debates, our perpetually- exquisite social events and an assemblage of talent from all around the world. We invite you to discover yourself in the sun-drenched boulevards of one of India’s most beautiful college campuses, into the irresistible allure exerted by the pocket-sized charms of Goa.

We look forward to seeing you in Goa in February, 2015. Stay tuned for more updates!

Sincerely,
Shreyash Mohatta
Dy. Secretary General
BITSMUN 2015
